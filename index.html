Best Time to Visit

🌤️

Oct – Feb

Peak Season

Cool & pleasant, 15–25°C. Ideal for sightseeing and outdoor activities.

☁️

Mar – May

Shoulder Season

Warm to hot, 25–40°C. Visit early mornings; enjoy fewer crowds.

🌧️

Jun – Sep

Monsoon Season

Hot & humid with occasional rain. Desert landscape turns lush green.

Travel Tips

Everything you need to know before visiting

✈️

Getting There

  • By Air: Jodhpur Airport (JDH) — direct flights from Delhi, Mumbai, Jaipur
  • By Train: Jodhpur Junction — connected to major Indian cities
  • By Road: NH-62, NH-112 — well-connected highway network
🚖

Getting Around

  • Auto-rickshaws — best for short distances, negotiate fare first
  • Ola/Uber — available in the city
  • Rental bikes & scooters — ideal for exploring independently
  • Cycle rickshaws — for the old city bazaars
💰

Budget Guide

  • Budget: ₹800–₹1,500/day (guesthouses, local food)
  • Mid-range: ₹3,000–₹6,000/day
  • Luxury: ₹10,000–₹50,000+/day (palace stays)
  • Entry fees: ₹100–₹600 per major attraction
🧳

What to Pack

  • Light cotton clothes (Oct–Mar), loose linen (summers)
  • Comfortable walking shoes for fort exploration
  • Sunscreen, sunglasses, and a hat
  • Scarf/dupatta for temple visits
🛡️

Safety & Etiquette

  • Remove shoes before entering temples
  • Dress modestly at religious sites
  • Ask permission before photographing locals
  • Carry small change for markets and auto fares
📱

Useful Info

  • Language: Hindi, Marwari, English widely understood
  • Currency: Indian Rupee (₹)
  • Emergency: Police 100 | Ambulance 108
  • Tourist Helpline: 1800-111-363
